Can I track my trades in real-time?

Yes! Our panel shows all open trades with live updates and detailed cards, so you never miss a market move.

How can I learn from my past performance?

With the trade history and smart journal, you can review each trade, identify strengths and weaknesses, and improve your strategy.

Can I manage my learning path and assignments?

Absolutely! The student roadmap and assignments section let you track progress, view instructor feedback, and monitor scores easily.

Definitely! The panel is fully responsive and bilingual, providing a smooth and complete experience on both mobile and desktop.
